June Update

Overview

  • Of the 232 budget documents monitored across all 29 countries, a total of 143 have now been published. This is a net increase of 2 from last month.
  • Of the 89 documents not publicly available, 37 were produced but not published, 9 were published late, and 43 were not produced at all.
  • 16 budget documents from 14 different countries were published in June. This includes 4 Audit Reports, 11 In-Year Reports, and 1 Year-End Report. These documents are all available on IBPâ€™s Open Budget Library.

Making progress

  • Mali has made its In-Year Report for Quarter 1 of 2015 publicly available. Mali had published the previous report too late to be considered publicly available.
  • Senegal has published its Audit Report for 2013. The Audit Report for the previous fiscal year had been published too late to be considered publicly available.

The Month Ahead

  • For countries with January to December fiscal years, Mid-Year Reviews for 2015 should be published in the coming month, along with Pre-Budget Statements for 2016.

All publicly available documents can be accessed through the Open Budget Library (www.openbudgetlibrary.org).
